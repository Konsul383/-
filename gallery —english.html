Jonathan "John" Locke is one of the surviving passengers who flew in the middle of Oceanic Flight 815. He was paralyzed in his lower torso, but after the disaster, the paralysis suddenly passed and this was the basis for Locke's belief that he has a special connection with the Island. Locke is sometimes compared to Benjamin Linus, as they both share similar traits.
